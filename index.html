What is WhiteFang?

WhiteFang is a group of working web development professionals and open source contributors that provide you with concise, information dense video courses on the best tools in the industry.

We are dedicated to respecting your time. This means that WhiteFang courses get to the point and deliver knowledge that you can use today. You won't find boring 8 hour courses on WhiteFang, instead you'll find a wealth of bite-sized courses that pack more information in a fraction of the time.
